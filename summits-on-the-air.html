Summits on the Air

Discover the exciting world of portable amateur radio operation from mountain summits around the globe.

About SOTA

Want to know more about the challenge of trying to communicate with hams who have trekked to summits of the world and set up their rigs. There are recognition awards and published lists of those who achieve stated goals in this adventure. Perhaps just the satisfaction of making contact with distant summits with a tiny low power radio. Or maybe you aspire to be one of those climbers!

So if we have piqued your interest, learn more at: http://www.w0c-sota.org/about-sota/
