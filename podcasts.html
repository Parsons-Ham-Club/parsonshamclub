Best Ham Radio Podcasts (2025)

The following is a list of some of the best ham radio podcasts to listen to in 2025, offering a mix of educational content, technical discussions, and personal stories from the ham radio community.

Ham Radio Crash Course

Hosted by Josh Nass KI6NAZ, this podcast covers suburban life, ham radio tips, practice exam questions, and longer topics. With nearly 400K subscribers, HRCC is one of the most popular ham radio channels.

ARRL Audio News

A weekly podcast of current news and events in amateur radio, prepared by ARRL The National Association for Amateur Radio. Each 10-15 minute episode can be aired over repeaters (no music included).

Ham Radio 360

Created by Cale Nelson K4CDN for the "New Guy," this podcast (2014-2018) produced 90+ shows with over 750,000 downloads. Topics range from setting up your first station to DX, antennas, and product reviews. The archive remains as a contribution to the hobby.

SolderSmoke Podcast

For 20 years, Bill Meara N2CQR and Pete N6QW have discussed workbench radio projects. Topics range from LTSpice to WSPR to QRSS to DSB, with a Mailbag segment bringing listener feedback and a sense of community.
